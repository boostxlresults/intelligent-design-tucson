## The Call of DIY AC Installation

It’s not hard to understand the appeal behind a DIY install: you get to skip the technician fees, and all the online tutorials make it look easier than it is. ## Risks of DIY AC Installation

### Incorrect AC Size

Choosing an AC that is too small or too large for your home can negatively impact efficiency. **Residential air conditioning in Tucson** must be sized properly to deal with the high desert heat. A poorly sized unit will do either one of two things. It will run inefficiently (read: spike your bills) or not cool your house effectively.

### Incorrect Refrigerant Charge

Air conditioning units operate at a delicate balance of refrigerant levels. If you add too much refrigerant to the system or too little, it can damage the compressor, which is one of the most expensive parts of the system to replace.

### Electrical & Safety Hazards

Improper handling of wiring and the like can lead to short circuits, potentially high voltage wiring to give electrical shock, and other fire hazards**. Air conditioning in Tucson** are high-voltage and should only be handled by a trained professional.

### Voiding Your Warranty

Most manufacturers require a professional to install the air conditioning system as part of the warranty requirements. If you do the job yourself, you would be liable to pay for future repairs.
