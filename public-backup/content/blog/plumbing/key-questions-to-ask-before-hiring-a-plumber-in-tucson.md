### Question #02 – Are You Licensed, Bonded, and Insured?

A full-service plumbing company will have all the necessary licenses, bonds, and insurance to protect you, your home, and your work. A plumbing license means that a company has passed the tests required by Tucson’s government to operate as a professional plumber.

Likewise, a bond guarantees payment when any dispute arises concerning the quality of service or payment for services rendered. And an insurance policy is necessary to protect both you and the plumber in case of any accidents or damage. Be sure to check for licenses, bonds, and insurance before hiring a plumber in Tucson. [Professional plumbers](https://www.idesignac.com/how-professional-plumbers-perform-drain-cleaning/) should not hesitate to provide proof that they are properly licensed and insured.

This will help protect you from any unexpected liabilities or damages that may occur during the plumbing project. Many licensed plumbers also have a guaranteed satisfaction policy, so you know that they stand behind their work. A reliable service with a track record of proven quality is what you should look for.

### Question #03: Are You a Member of Any Professional Associations or Organizations?

A quality plumbing professional should be a member of professional trade organizations, such as Plumbers Local Union No. 790 or the National Association of Plumbing-Heating-Cooling Contractors (PHCC). Membership in these professional organizations means that the plumber has received comprehensive training and is up-to-date on industry standards and best practices.

Tucson plumbers who are members of these organizations also demonstrate commitment to their craft by maintaining professional qualifications and actively taking part in continuing education. Your plumbing system is a complex network of pipes, fixtures, and appliances.

It’s important to make sure the plumber you hire is qualified and knowledgeable about plumbing codes and standards in Tucson. Asking whether the plumber is a member of a professional organization is one way to ensure that he or she has access to current information about best practices in the trade.

It is important to ask the plumber in Tucson what types of materials they use for their jobs. Some plumbers may be using outdated products or tools that could lead to further damage or injury down the line. [Plumbing issues](https://www.idesignac.com/what-are-the-most-common-plumbing-issues-in-homes/) can be serious and you want to make sure the plumber is taking the necessary steps to ensure that your plumbing issues are adequately addressed. Here are some example questions to ask:

*   Are the products used up to current codes and standards?
*   Do you use copper, galvanized steel, or PVC pipe for plumbing jobs?
*   What type of fittings do you use when connecting pipes?
*   Do you use lead-free solder when soldering pipes together?
*   What type of tools do you use to unclog drains?

Your plumber should have an answer for each of these questions, and they should be able to explain the types of materials they use in detail.
