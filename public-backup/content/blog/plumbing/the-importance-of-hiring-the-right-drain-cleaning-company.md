### Why Hiring the Right Drain Cleaning Company is Important

As we mentioned, drain clogs can be a huge headache. They can cause water to back up in your sink or shower, making it difficult to use them. In some cases, clogged drains can also lead to flooding. Not only is this a mess, but it can also cause significant damage to your home.

That’s why hiring a drain cleaning company is crucial to getting the job done right. In terms of addressing these problems, here are the things the right company can provide:

*   **Right tools for the job:** Drain cleaning companies have specialized equipment that can quickly and effectively clear clogged drains. They don’t use materials that will make the process more complex, like soap scum and hot water.
*   **Proper knowledge:** Drain cleaning companies are trained to safely and effectively clean drains. They will also be able to identify potential problems with your drain before they become an issue.
*   **Right experience:** Drain cleaning companies have years of experience dealing with drain issues. This means they know how to quickly and effectively solve the problem.
*   **Provide preventative maintenance:** Drain cleaning companies can also provide preventative maintenance to help keep your drain from becoming clogged again. For instance, they will tell you to avoid pouring food particles down the drain as they will end up in our water supply.
*   **They offer a warranty:** The right cleaning company can stand behind their work with a warranty. So, if the problem arises, they will come back and fix it at no cost to you.
*   **Well aware of city codes:** Drain cleaning companies are required to follow city codes when it comes to drain cleaning. This ensures that the job is done safely and correctly.
*   **They know the drain line:** The best drain cleaning companies will understand how your drain is connected to the rest of the plumbing in your home. This allows them to quickly and easily identify any potential problems.
*   **They are licensed and insured:** The right drain cleaning company has all the necessary licenses and insurance. This protects you in case something goes wrong during the drain cleaning process.
*   **They clean their mess:** Drain cleaning companies will clean up any mess they make while working on your drain. They know the right garbage disposal procedures to ensure that your drain is left clean and clear.
*   **They don’t use harsh chemicals**: Some [drain cleaners](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Drain_cleaner) can be very harsh on your clogged drain. This can damage your pipes and lead to even more problems down the road. The right drain cleaning company will use safe, gentle products that won’t damage your drain.

### How the Right Cleaning Company Conducts Business

Now that you know why hiring the right drain cleaning company is important, let’s take a look at how the right company conducts business. Here are a few things you can expect from the right drain cleaning company:

#### 1\. Give you an Estimate

When looking for drain cleaning services, we always hope for a company that is upfront and honest about its pricing. The best drain cleaning companies will be able to give you an estimate of the cost before they start the job. This way, you can budget for the service and know what to expect.

#### 2\. Show Up on Time

After you’ve scheduled an appointment, the last thing you want is for the drain cleaning company to show up late. The best drain cleaning companies will be on time and ready to work.

#### 3\. Work Quickly and Efficiently

The best drain cleaning companies understand that your time is valuable. They will work quickly and efficiently to get the job done as soon as possible.

#### 4\. Be Professional

When the drain cleaning company arrives, they will be professional and courteous. They will also take the time to answer any questions you may have about the process. As a client, you have the right to ask as many questions as you want, and the drain cleaning company should be more than happy to answer them.

#### 5\. Give you a Written Agreement

As you ask them, they will give you a written agreement. This document will outline the scope of work and the price. Be sure to read it over carefully before signing. The written agreement should have the following information:

*   The name and contact information of the drain cleaning company.
*   A description of the work to be performed.
*   The price of the service.
*   The date the work will be performed.
*   The signature of the drain cleaning company representative.

#### 6\. Conduct Inspection

Once you’ve signed the agreement, the drain cleaning company will get to work. They will start by inspecting the drain, and then they will begin the process of clearing the clog. During this time, they will keep you updated on their progress.

#### 7\. Will Perform a Test

After the drain has been cleared, the drain cleaning company will perform a test to ensure that the clog is gone. They will also check for any potential problems that could cause the drain to become clogged again in the future. They will check all the drain lines and kitchen sink to ensure they are clear.

#### 8\. Will Clean Up After Themselves

Once the job is done, the drain cleaning company will clean up after themselves. This means they will dispose of any waste properly and leave your home as clean as they found it.
