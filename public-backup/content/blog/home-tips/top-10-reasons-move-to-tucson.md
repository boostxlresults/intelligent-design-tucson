## Moving to Tucson: What You Need to Know

### Climate Considerations

While Tucson's weather is generally fantastic, new residents should know:

- **Summer heat is real:** Plan indoor activities mid-day
- **Monsoon season (July-September):** Dramatic storms
- **Dry air:** Stay hydrated, use moisturizer
- **High UV exposure:** Sunscreen is essential
- **Low humidity:** Great for those with allergies

- **Quality air conditioning** is non-negotiable
