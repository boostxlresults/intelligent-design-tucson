# Service Location Expansion Blueprint
## Target: 720-880 Lines Per File (Safety Margin Above 650 Minimum)

## REQUIRED SECTIONS WITH TARGET LINE COUNTS:

### 1. INTRODUCTION (50-70 lines)
- Multi-paragraph detailed introduction (4-5 paragraphs)
- Location-specific details (community characteristics, climate, housing types)
- Service area coverage details
- Trust signals (22,000+ reviews, family-owned, veteran-owned, BBB A+)
- 3-4 internal links integrated naturally

### 2. WHY CHOOSE INTELLIGENT DESIGN (60-80 lines)
- 6-8 detailed paragraphs
- ServiceTitan metrics (73-min avg arrival, 42 vehicles, 80% same-visit repair)
- Proof points (68 NATE techs, 17+ years avg experience, #1 for 7 years)
- Location-specific advantages
- 2-3 internal links

### 3. COMPREHENSIVE SERVICE OFFERINGS (80-100 lines)
- Detailed multi-paragraph descriptions (NOT bullet lists)
- Service categories with rich explanations
- Equipment types covered
- Emergency vs. scheduled service details
- Warranty information
- 3-4 internal links

### 4. THREE DETAILED CASE STUDIES (360-400 lines total = 120-135 lines EACH)
**Each case study MUST include:**
- Specific neighborhood/location within service area
- Detailed challenge description (2-3 paragraphs)
- Solution narrative (3-4 paragraphs with technical details)
- Timeframe and team details
- Equipment specifications
- Multi-paragraph customer testimonial quote
- Detailed results array (10-12 specific metrics):
  * Cost savings ($ and %)
  * Efficiency improvements (%)
  * Energy reduction (kWh or BTU)
  * Comfort metrics
  * ROI timeframe
  * Environmental impact
  * System lifespan
  * Reliability improvements
  * Safety enhancements
  * Customer satisfaction details

### 5. TEN-STEP HOWTO GUIDE (150-180 lines)
**"How to Prepare Your [Location] Home for [Service] Call"**
- 10 detailed steps (NOT 8)
- Each step: 2-3 paragraphs with specific instructions
- Include what to expect, what to prepare, questions to ask
- Location-specific considerations
- Pro tips and warnings

### 6. COMPREHENSIVE PRICING BREAKDOWN (100-120 lines)
**Multiple pricing tables:**
- Service category comparison table (3-4 categories)
- Equipment cost ranges (Small/Medium/Large)
- Maintenance plans comparison
- Financing options breakdown
- Emergency vs. scheduled pricing
- Seasonal promotions/offers
- Payment methods accepted
- Warranty coverage details

### 7. EQUIPMENT COMPARISON SECTION (80-100 lines)
**Detailed comparison tables:**
- Efficiency ratings (SEER, AFUE, EER, etc.)
- System types comparison (single-stage vs. two-stage vs. variable-capacity)
- Brand recommendations
- Size/capacity guidelines
- Feature comparisons
- Lifespan expectations
- Warranty differences

### 8. SEASONAL MAINTENANCE GUIDE (100-120 lines)
**Month-by-month or season-by-season breakdown:**
- Spring preparation (3-4 paragraphs)
- Summer operation tips (3-4 paragraphs)
- Fall winterization (3-4 paragraphs)
- Winter maintenance (3-4 paragraphs)
- Why annual maintenance matters (2-3 paragraphs)
- Climate-specific considerations for Tucson

### 9. REPAIR VS. REPLACE DECISION GUIDE (60-80 lines)
- Age of equipment considerations
- $5,000 rule explanation
- Efficiency comparisons
- Cost-benefit analysis
- Rebate/incentive considerations
- Emergency replacement scenarios
- Decision tree/flowchart narrative

### 10. EMERGENCY SERVICE PROTOCOLS (50-70 lines)
- When to call for emergency service
- What qualifies as emergency vs. routine
- Response time guarantees
- Emergency decision tree
- Safety considerations
- After-hours availability
- Location-specific emergency contacts

### 11. LOCAL CLIMATE CONSIDERATIONS (40-60 lines)
- Tucson-specific weather patterns
- Location microclimate details
- Seasonal challenges
- Equipment recommendations for climate
- Energy efficiency in desert environment

### 12. WARRANTY & FINANCING INFORMATION (40-60 lines)
- Warranty coverage details
- Extended warranty options
- Financing terms
- Payment plans
- Special offers for location
- Service agreements

### 13. INTERNAL LINKING (14+ LINKS THROUGHOUT)
**Distribute links naturally across ALL sections above:**
- Primary service pages (3-4 links)
- Related services (4-5 links)
- Location pages (2-3 links)
- About/reviews/financing pages (3-4 links)
